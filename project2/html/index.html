The Rise of Khabib Nurmagomedov

Khabib Nurmagomedov is a famous mixed martial artist from Dagestan, Russia. He is best known for his dominance in the UFC lightweight division and his undefeated professional record. Khabib became a global sports icon because of his discipline, strength, and unique wrestling-based fighting style. Born on September 20, 1988, Khabib was trained by his father, Abdulmanap Nurmagomedov, from a very young age. He learned wrestling, sambo, and judo, which helped him control opponents inside the octagon. His calm mindset and strong work ethic made him different from many other fighters.
